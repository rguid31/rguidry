My Story

My journey began in chemical engineering, where I developed a strong foundation in systematic problem-solving and technical analysis. During my studies at Louisiana State University, I discovered my passion for data analysis and its power to transform complex information into actionable insights.

What started as a curiosity about process optimization and data-driven decision making evolved into a career transition. I recognized that my engineering background, combined with my growing expertise in data analysis, positioned me uniquely to bridge the gap between technical implementation and strategic business decisions.

Today, I leverage this unique combination of skills to help organizations transform their data into strategic advantages, while maintaining the precision and methodical approach I developed as an engineer.
